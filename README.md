Logo Explanation:

The logo for the Institute of Digital Risk is built around a geometric cube that integrates the letters "IDR" within its structure. 
The cube symbolizes stability, structure, and the multi-layered nature of digital risk management.
Each visible side represents different dimensions of digital risk such as cybersecurity, technology governance, and data protection.
The bold orange color reflects innovation, energy, and forward-thinking technology, while the minimal design ensures clarity and scalability across digital platforms like websites and mobile interfaces.
Overall, the logo communicates a modern, secure, and technology-driven identity aligned with the institute’s mission.
